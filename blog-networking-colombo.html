Building Professional Networks in Colombo

Practical, culturally aware strategies to grow meaningful connections

Why Networking Still Matters

Despite digital recruitment, referrals and trusted introductions remain one of the strongest hiring channels in Sri Lanka.

High-Value Event Types

  • Tech meetups (DevOps, JavaScript, Cloud)
  • Chamber of Commerce sessions
  • University alumni gatherings
  • Co-working community demos

Conversation Starters

Keep it genuine: “What projects are you working on this quarter?” vs “Can you get me a job?”

Follow-Up System

Log contacts in a simple sheet: name, context, value you can offer, next touch date.

Give Before You Ask

Share an article, refer a candidate, offer lightweight feedback—create relational equity.

Avoid Common Mistakes

  • Spamming LinkedIn requests without a note
  • Only contacting people when you need help
  • Ignoring cross-language opportunities (Sinhala/Tamil/English)

Convert Relationships into Opportunities

Use your network insights to target roles others miss.
