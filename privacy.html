Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 8, 2025

JobPortal LK ("we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

  • Usage data such as pages visited, time on site, device and browser details.
  • Search and filter preferences related to job browsing.
  • Contact information you provide (e.g., when contacting us).

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and remember preferences. Third parties (including Google) may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this or other websites.

Google AdSense and DoubleClick Cookie

Google uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google’s use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting YourAdChoices.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the website.
  • To improve usability and content relevance.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers for analytics and advertising under strict agreements.

Your Choices

  • Cookie Preferences: Manage via the cookie banner or your browser settings.
  • Personalized Ads: Opt out at Google Ads Settings.

Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@jobportallk.example.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
